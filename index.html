About Us

With glorious heritage of auspicious presence blessings of Mahaprabhu Sri Chaitanyadev and flourished by creative touch of Sahitya Samrat Bankim Chandra Chattopadhya, served as magistrate in Baruipur.Baruipur High school was established in 1858, a year after Sepoy Mutiny. As a student of this school, a pioneering and one of the oldest educational institute of South 24 Pgs(WB), we have shared our child & boyhood, education, game, emotion, love and unbound friendship together here in Baruipur.

PAST EVENTS

COVID-19 RELIEF PROGRAMME
A month long COVID-19 relief programme has been successfully organised in Baruipur Municipality area from 10.05.2020 to 06.06.2020. We have distributed food grains & other grocery items to more than 500 families including 90 families in Baruipur red light area. It was a unique experience and privilege for BHS95 to organise Relief programme in CORONA Pandemic. 

Details

AMPHEN RELIEF PROGRAMME
On 27.06.2020, members of BHS95 reached at Rangabelia Village of Gosabe Block, Sundarban with Relief items and distributed saree, ORS and dry food among Cyclone Amphen affected more than 300 families. This also a unique experience for us, as we have distributed Relief for the first time in any cyclone/flood affected area.
