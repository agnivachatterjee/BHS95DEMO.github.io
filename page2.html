ABOUT US

With glorious heritage of auspicious presence blessings of Mahaprabhu Sri Chaitanyadev and flourished by creative touch of Sahitya Samrat Bankim Chandra Chattopadhya, served as magistrate in Baruipur. Baruipur High school was established in 1858, a year after Sepoy Mutiny. As a student of this school, a pioneering and one of the oldest educational institute of South 24 Pgs(WB), we have shared our child & boyhood, education, game, emotion, love and unbound friendship together here in Baruipur.

With the illustrious presence and co-operation of eminent personalities like – Nobel laureate Mother Teressa and other distinguish dignitaries; the school has enriched and attended its zenith since the inception in State education boards. With acclaimed academic excellence, alumni of this esteemed institute are pervaded in various fields and respectable posts in both Govt. and private sectors in India and aboard. 

We, the pass out students of 1995 batch of Baruipur High School are privileged to form a trust “BARUIPUR HIGH SCHOOL 95 BATCH (BHS 95)” for a object of education, relief of the poor, rural development, medical relief and other related charitable works. Trust activities have been divided into two part – Long Term Projection & Short Term Projection. Establishment of an Educational hub (General, technical skill development etc.), a Holistic Medical facility (mainly for under privileged) and Social Upliftment Movement will come under our long term projection. Where rural development and other related activities will be a part of our short term and continuous work projection.
